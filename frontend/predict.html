Exponential Smoothing Forecast

Adaptive Earnings Forecast — Exponential Smoothing
Adjust α to weight recent shifts more (high α) or smooth noise (low α)
Exponential Smoothing
Formula: Sₜ = α·Xₜ + (1−α)·Sₜ₋₁. A high α (close to 1) makes the forecast react quickly to recent changes. A low α smooths over noise. Unlike a simple Moving Average, older data is never abruptly dropped — it decays exponentially.
